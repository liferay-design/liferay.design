Access these templates in [Google Docs](https://docs.google.com/).

**To display template gallery:**

<Image
	dropShadow
	rounded
	size="medium"
	src="/images/blueprints/resources/display-templates-in-docs.png"
	caption=""
	/>

1. In [Google Docs](https://docs.google.com/), open the hamburger menu in the top left corner and select "Settings".

2. Check "Display recent templates on home screens".

3. Click "Ok."

**To use a template:**

1. Click "Template gallery" in the top right of the templates section.

2. Use any of the approved templates in the liferay.com tab under \*Liferay Corporate Templates*.
