## Our Team

Our team is diverse. We trully believe this is a good quality for design teams, people coming from different background that can offer diverse range of points of views.

## What do we expect from you?

When you apply we expect to see your **CV** and **portfolio**.

Something we really appreciate in your portfolio are stories, try to answer these questions in your projects:
- What did I do in the project?
- What was the improvement compared to previous solution?
- How I ended up in this result? Why?
- What adversities did I overcome (if any)?
- What did I learn (if any)?

**Make your portfolio remarkable**, it will impact more and it will be easier to remember.

Please, maintain both documents updated. It help us to speed up the process.

## Our Interview Process

Our hiring process is simple

0. Candidate CV & Portfolio review
1. HR Interview
2. Portfolio interview
3. Live exercise
    * Short theoretical test with questions about visual, interaction, research and strategy.
    * Live exercise
4. Interview with our design director

## What can you expect from us?

We have been in your situation several times and we know how it feels.

Hiring processes are not easy, they can have many steps, sometimes they generete uncertainty, illusion, happiness, but also anxiety. We know this is a rollercoaster of feelings, but please believe that we don't like to play with your feelings. 

From our side, *we commit to contact you contact you back offering feedback about your application*.

Remarkable profiles are easier to remember. Everyday there are new job offers in the market and many times we know designers in those companies. If we see the offer and we think you can match, we will let you or the company know about the other.
