## Primary Channels

**_All designers_** _should_ **_actively participate_** _in these channels._

1.  [**#d-design**](https://liferay.slack.com/messages/CL4FSBYE4) — ![:mega:](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7) **global announcements** _— anything posted should be relevant to the whole department_
2.  [**#des-ops**](https://liferay.slack.com/messages/CL2KU6A65) — **operational information** — _everything tool or process related_
3.  [**#des-feedback**](https://liferay.slack.com/messages/CLARSSP9D) — **publicly share work in progress** _— documents, wireframes, mockups, comps, etc_
4.  [**#s-liferay-design**](https://liferay.slack.com/messages/CL86Q8AKS) _— updates and discussion related to https://liferay.design_
5.  [**#t-lexicon**](https://liferay.slack.com/messages/CKT3L1G58) _— updates and discussion related to Lexicon_

## Secondary Channels
