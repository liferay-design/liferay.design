### Advice for ambitious designers?

Go for it! Find what you are more passionated to do and focus on this in the first moment. Much high-quality free content is available on the internet, also new tools are always appearing, stay aware of these.  From time to time, revisit your old works. If you see the potential for an increase, it means that you're already making improvements.
